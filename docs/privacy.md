# Capable — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-04-25_

Capable is a personal fitness tracker. It is designed to work entirely on
your device, with no account, no server, and no analytics.

## What data does Capable collect?

**None.** Capable does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
Everything you log — workouts, programs, sessions, personal records,
settings — is stored locally on your device using the operating
system's standard secure storage (`AsyncStorage` / `UserDefaults`).

We do not run a server. We do not have a database. The app does not
talk to any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting
service.

## What permissions does Capable use?

- **Notifications** (optional). If you grant permission, Capable
  schedules a single local notification when your rest timer ends so
  you can put the phone down between sets. The notification is
  scheduled locally on your device — nothing is sent to a server.

You can deny or revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings →
Notifications → Capable. Denying it will not break any other feature;
the rest timer will still count down inside the app, you just won't
get a notification.

## Sharing and exports

Capable lets you export a JSON backup of your data using the standard
iOS share sheet. This is a one-shot, user-initiated action — Capable
does not upload your data anywhere. Wherever you choose to send the
file (Files, AirDrop, an email, etc.) is governed by that destination's
privacy policy, not ours.

## Children

Capable is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly
collect data from anyone (since we collect nothing at all).

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of Capable introduces any data collection (it
shouldn't, but we want to be honest if priorities change), we will
update this document and surface the change in-app before that
version ships.
