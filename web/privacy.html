Capable privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-04-23

The short version

Capable is a local workout tracker. Everything you enter — your workouts, programs, logged sessions, personal records, and settings — is stored only on the device where you use the app. Capable has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. Nothing you enter leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

What we store, and where

  • Workouts, programs, logged sessions, personal records, achievements, and settings are written to your device's on-device storage (AsyncStorage under the key capable.store.v2).
  • No data is transmitted to Capable or to any third party. We do not operate any backend.

What we don't collect

  • No account, email, or phone number.
  • No analytics, crash reports, or telemetry.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No location data.
  • No health data from Apple Health or Google Fit.

Permissions the app may ask for

  • Notifications — used only to fire your rest-timer alert when the app is backgrounded. Notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on-device.

Export and deletion

You can export a JSON backup of your data at any time from Settings → Data. You can remove everything Capable has stored on your device via Settings → Danger zone → Wipe account data, or by uninstalling the app.

Children

Capable is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
